Welcome to Geologist's Recipes: Unearth the Power of Data Analysis with R!

If you have a passion for geology and a thirst for knowledge, you've come to the right place. This webpage is dedicated to the fascinating world of geological data analysis, where we harness the incredible capabilities of the R programming language to explore the Earth's secrets...

If your data behaves like a uncontroled teenager, you came to the right place. With these recipes we will help you to handle your data making the most out of it with inovative and state-of-the-art tools that even a geologist like me can understand...
